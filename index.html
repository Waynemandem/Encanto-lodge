Our Story

A little piece of
Mexico, in Lagos

"Somewhere between terracotta walls and Lagos sunsets, we built a world worth escaping into."

Encanto Lodge was born from a love of two cultures — the warmth of Mexican haciendas and the electrifying spirit of Lagos. Tucked away on Younis Bashorun Street in Victoria Island, our lodge is a sanctuary of bold flavours, lush gardens, shimmering pools, and rooms designed for deep rest.

Whether you're staying for a night or a week, you'll find that time moves differently here. Slower. Softer. Exactly as it should.
